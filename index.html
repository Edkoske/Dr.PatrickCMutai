About Dr. Patrick Mutai

Current role: Speaker, County Assembly of Kericho.

Dr. Patrick C. Mutai combines principled leadership with a track record of measurable results. He has guided legislative reforms that improved budget transparency, strengthened oversight, and expanded participatory governance across Kericho County.

As a committed public servant, Dr. Mutai prioritizes community-driven development, investments in agriculture and youth employment, and partnerships that deliver sustainable services to families and local enterprises.

Core values: Integrity • Transparency • Service • Unity

Leadership & Achievements

  • Budget transparency: Led initiatives that increased public access to budget information and improved fiscal oversight.
  • Governance reforms: Championed inclusive policies to strengthen citizen participation and institutional accountability.
  • Community development: Advanced targeted investments in roads, water, health facilities and youth skills programmes.
  • Consensus building: Facilitated cross-party cooperation to pass critical development legislation.

Vision for Kericho 2027

A hopeful and prosperous Kericho where every community shares in opportunity and progress. Dr. Mutai's vision focuses on sustainable economic growth, modern agriculture, resilient infrastructure and healthcare, strong education outcomes, and transparent, accountable government.

His plan centers on people-first policies that unlock investment, create dignified jobs for youth, and deliver essential services more efficiently and equitably.

Economic empowerment

Expand access to finance and markets, support SMEs and cooperatives, and build resilient value chains that keep more revenue in local communities.

Agriculture & youth

Boost productivity through extension services, climate-smart farming and agribusiness training — with clear pathways for youth employment in the sector.

Infrastructure & health

Deliver reliable roads, safe water, and strengthened primary health services so families can thrive.

Education & governance

Raise education standards, support teachers, and embed transparency across public institutions to restore trust.
